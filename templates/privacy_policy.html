Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how we (LuciusTensors) collect, use, and share information when you use our website that offers AI products powered by the OpenAI API.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal user data on our website. Our services are powered by the OpenAI API, and as such, any data generated or processed by the API is subject to OpenAI's data handling policies.

2. OpenAI API Data Handling

Our website utilizes the OpenAI API for AI product functionality. OpenAI may securely retain API inputs and outputs for up to 30 days to provide services and identify abuse. After 30 days, API inputs and outputs are removed from OpenAI systems unless legally required to retain them. You can refer to OpenAI's Platform Docs page for more details on data handling.

3. Access to Stored Data

Access to API business data stored on OpenAI systems is limited to authorized employees who require access for engineering support, investigating potential platform abuse, and legal compliance. Additionally, specialized third-party contractors may have access, bound by confidentiality and security obligations, solely for reviewing abuse and misuse.

4. Security Measures

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect the information processed through the OpenAI API. However, it's essential to be aware that data transmitted over the internet is not entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the security of information transmitted to or from our website.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. The most recent version of the Privacy Policy will be available on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at luciustensors@gmail.com.
